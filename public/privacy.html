Privacy Policy

Postly helps users create product posts and publish them to connected social media and marketplace accounts.

Data We Collect

We store account email, product content, uploaded media, generated post text, publishing statuses, shop settings, and social access tokens after the user connects a platform.

How We Use Data

We use this data only to provide the service: generate content, save drafts, publish posts, schedule posts, and show connection status.

Social Platform Tokens

Facebook, Instagram, and other social tokens are stored per user and are used only to publish content or verify account connection. Tokens are not sold or shared with third parties.

Data Deletion

Users can request deletion on the Data Deletion page or delete their account from the app when available.
