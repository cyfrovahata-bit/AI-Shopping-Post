Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your Postly account and all associated data at any time: your account, uploaded products and media references, generated posts and publishing history, shop settings, and every connected-platform token (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Telegram, Prom.ua, OLX.ua, Rozetka).

How To Request Deletion

Option 1 — In the app: log in, open Settings, and use the account deletion action. This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all related data.

Option 2 — By email: send an email to info@cyfrovahata.com.ua from the email address registered on your Postly account, with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will confirm deletion within a reasonable time.

Deleted accounts and data cannot be restored.

Facebook / Meta Data Deletion Callback

Meta may call our automated deletion callback at /api/data-deletion when a user removes Postly from their Facebook account. The endpoint returns a confirmation code and a link back to this page, as required by Meta's Platform Terms.

TikTok Data Deletion

If you disconnect Postly from your TikTok account (via TikTok's app settings or Postly's Settings page), Postly deletes the associated TikTok access/refresh tokens immediately. You can also request full account deletion as described above.
